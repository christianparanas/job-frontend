Information about the system

- Our platform is a cutting-edge job management system designed to streamline the hiring process for employers. Built with Angular and PrimeNG, it offers a user-friendly interface for creating, managing, and tracking job postings. With features like skill-based filtering, real-time status updates, and a responsive design, we aim to connect employers with top talent efficiently and effectively. This system was developed as part of a collaborative effort to innovate recruitment technology, launched on March 7, 2025.
